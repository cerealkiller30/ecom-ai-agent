RETURN & REFUND POLICY
======================
All products can be returned within 30 days of delivery.
Electronics must be returned in original packaging with all accessories.
Clothing and footwear can be returned if unused and tags are intact.
Damaged or defective items can be returned at any time within 90 days.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
Cash on delivery orders are refunded via bank transfer.

CANCELLATION POLICY
===================
Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.
Once an order is shipped, it cannot be cancelled — initiate a return instead.
Cancelled orders are refunded within 3-5 business days.
To cancel, provide your order number to customer support.

SHIPPING POLICY
===============
Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days.
Express delivery takes 1-2 business days and costs Rs. 99 extra.
Free shipping on orders above Rs. 999.
Orders are shipped within 24 hours of placement.
Tracking information is sent via SMS and email after shipping.

DAMAGED ITEMS POLICY
====================
If your item arrives damaged, report it within 48 hours of delivery.
Take a photograph of the damaged item and packaging.
A replacement will be shipped within 2-3 business days.
Alternatively, a full refund can be issued within 5 business days.
